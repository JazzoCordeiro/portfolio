My Journey to Becoming a Frontend Developer - My Free Roadmap

It's true that during my journey and my degree, I participated in some bootcamps and took a few specific courses from universities and paid sites, but throughout my studies, I gathered playlists of lessons, course links, articles with explanations, and other important resources that helped me build my foundation, better understand this world, and strengthen my knowledge. Here you’ll find all kinds of information, from basic programming logic to advanced resources or channels teaching useful day-to-day frontend skills.

I recommend this list to anyone who wants to become a frontend developer. The material is extremely rich and extensive. Most of the content is in Portuguese, but there are also some resources in English and Spanish, and a few even in multiple languages.

In addition to this material, my tip is: Continuously search for BOOTCAMPS on the internet. From time to time, there are free bootcamps!

Hope this helps!
